Just Bring me to the game!

What is it?

In short its an endless choose your own adventure book. Any registered player can got through a story and either make to the end by winning/dying. If he reaches a point where there is no story he can create content and tow new choices for the empty choice he selected.

Is it endless?

Somewhat. It can go on forever, however if all paths are blocked by an end game message their will be no way to continue the story.
